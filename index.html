Project
Deep Learning

Master

Lensless facial recognition with encrypted optics and a neural network computation

Conventional lens-based imagery keeps the spatial fidelity with respect to the object, thus, leading to the privacy concerns. We employed a coded mask as the encryption scheme, which allows a readily noninterpretable representation on the sensor. A deep neural network computation was used to extract the features and further conduct the identification.
